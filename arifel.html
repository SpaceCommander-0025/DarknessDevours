Backstory

Born in Howlspear, Arifel found her calling through the arcane arts and natural magic. At reaching the age of 15 and being a fresh adult, she began her spirtual journey and traveled across several southern cities and villages to expand her knowledge of the multiverse and learn all she can. Not long after she started traveling, she began having dreams of a dark, smokey figure in the shape of a young woman. The dreams vary in severity often dealing with death in some shape or form. After meeting Riddick Alexsander at Kelmhagen where she was studying for a short time, he asked her to stop by Gravemarch if she ever made it that far North. After traveling farther North, her dreams got more vivid, clearer and opened the ability to psychically chat with Cienna. This allowed her to find Cienna Elarocen and meet in Gravemarch. Afterwards, over the next few years, they became best friends. When she stays in Gravemarch and attends Mortalis Collegium, she is under the tutalage of Riddick Alexsander who has taken a liking to her. He often has her go on short adventures to expand her knowledge and Cienna is always sending her letters.
