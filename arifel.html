Overview

Name: Seraphine Duskwhisper

Background: Haunted One

Backstory

Born under a blood moon in the cursed village of Draymoor, Seraphine made a pact with an ancient entity known only as "The Whispering Blade." Haunted by shadows only she can see, she roams Aerinthal seeking vengeance for a family she can no longer remember.
